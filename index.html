Kashmiri Chicken Biryani

Biryani, with its vibrant hues and tantalizing aroma, boasts an appearance as captivating as its taste. The dish presents a mesmerizing amalgamation of long-grain rice, fragrant spices, and tenderly cooked meat or vegetables, adorned with a spectrum of colors and textures. Each layer tells a story—the rice, infused with saffron, turmeric, or rose water, forms a canvas for the flavorful ensemble. Its significance transcends mere sustenance; biryani embodies tradition, heritage, and a culinary journey through time
